# Year 9 Summer Exam Information (2025–26)
## AQA GCSE Chemistry (Single Science)

## Exam Details
- Date: 06 May
- Time: 09:05
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Format: One written paper

The exam will cover the following topics:

## Year 9 Topics

### 1. Atomic Structure, Bonding & Properties

- Names and symbols of elements
- Atomic model
- Isotopes
- Electron arrangement
- How electron arrangement links to the periodic table
- Forming ions
- Ionic bonding
- Properties of ionic structures
- Covalent bonding
- Properties of covalent structures
- Giant covalent structures
- Nanoparticles
- Metallic structures
- Properties of metals and non-metals
- Steel and alloys
- Corrosion
- History of the atomic model
- Development of the periodic table

### 2. Formulae & Equations

- Names and formulae of ionic compounds
- Names and formulae of covalent compounds
- Word equations
- Conservation of mass
- Balancing equations
- Converting word equations into balanced equations

### Names and Symbols of Elements:

*Atom* - smallest part of an element that can exist
*Element* - contains only 1 type of atom
↳ a symbol of an element represents one atom of said element; or natural molecular form
*Molecule* - a number of atoms bonded together
↳ halogens are so reactive that they bond with themselves forming *diatomic* molecules ($O_2$, $I_2$, etc.)
*Compound* - 2 or more elements chemically bonded in a fixed ratio (meaning $H_2O$ will never be $H_4O_2$)

### Atomic Model:
| Particle | Place in atom | Mass (relative) | Charge (relative) |
|:--------:|:-------------:|:---------------:|:-----------------:|
| proton   | inside        | 1               | +1                |
| neutron  | inside        | 1               | 0                 |
| electron | in shells     | 1/2000          | -1                |
The radius of an atom is $0.1 \mu m$ - $1 * 10^{-10}m$
The radius of an atom's nucleus is $1/10000$th of an atom - $1 * 10^{-14}m$

$No_{protons} = No_{electrons}$ because an atom is neutral in charge by definition.

*Electron* - subatomic particle with a charge -1. They orbit the nucleus in electron shells due to electrostatic forces of attraction.

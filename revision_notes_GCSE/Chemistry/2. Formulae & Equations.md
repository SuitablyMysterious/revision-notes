### Balancing:

In a chemical equation the products need to match to the reactants. For example:
$$Mg(NO_3)_2 + NaOH \to Mg(OH)_2+NaNO_3$$
Reactants:
1 $Magnesium$ ion
2 $Nitrate$ ions
1 $Sodium$ ion
1 $Hydroxide$ ion

Products:
1 $Magnesium$ ion
2 $Hydroxide$ ions
1 $Sodium$ ion
1 $Nitrate$ ion
$$Mg(NO_3)_2 + 2NaOH \to Mg(OH)_2+2NaNO_3$$
Reactants:
1 $Magnesium$ ion
2 $Nitrate$ ions
2 $Sodium$ ion
2 $Hydroxide$ ion

Products:
1 $Magnesium$ ion
2 $Nitrate$ ions
2 $Sodium$ ion
2 $Hydroxide$ ion

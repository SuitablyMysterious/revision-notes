### Transverse Waves:

![[Pasted image 20260414184436.png]]
Oscillations in transverse waves are perpendicular to the direction of travel.




Light is a transverse wave (although can be classed as a particle with particle-wave duality) that travels at $3*10^8 ms^{-1}$.

#### Equations:
$$Frequency = Period^{-1}$$
$$Period = Frequency^{-1}$$

$$Frequency = Number_{Waves}*Time^{-1}$$
$$F = N_wT^{-1}$$
